Essential Travel Tips

Hard-won lessons from years on the road — so you can travel smarter from day one.

🧳

Pack Light

One carry-on changes everything. Roll your clothes, choose neutral colours, and pack half of what you think you need.

💳

Avoid Foreign Fees

Use a no-fee travel card and always pay in the local currency to avoid hidden conversion charges.

🗺️

Plan Loosely

Book your first and last night. Leave the rest open. The best experiences are the ones you stumble upon.

🛡️

Get Insurance

Travel insurance is not optional. A single hospital visit abroad can cost more than your entire trip budget.
